Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how "OhaPet" (hereinafter referred to as "the App") handles user's personal information and data.

1. Information Collected and Purpose of Use

The App acquires and uses the following information to improve user's sleep habits and provide a pet raising simulation experience.

  • Sleep/Wake data: Alarm stop time, turn-off/wake-up action times within the app.
  • In-app behavior history: History of pet care (use of food, toys, etc.).
[IMPORTANT]
All these user data (sleep records and raising data) are saved locally on the user's device. The developer (Rhythmap) never sends, collects, or views this personal data on external servers.

2. Information Collection Modules (Ads/Analytics)

To maintain the free provision of the service and analyze app usage (quality improvement), the App uses third-party services (Google AdMob, Firebase Analytics). These services may automatically acquire the following information to display ads according to user interests and improve the app:

  • Device information (OS version, device model, etc.)
  • IP address and approximate location information
  • Advertising identifiers (iOS IDFA, Android AAID, etc.)
  • In-app usage status and operation logs (including crash information)

For the acquisition and use of this information, please refer to the privacy policy below:
Google Privacy Policy

3. Data Storage and Deletion

Data is stored on the user's device. By uninstalling the app, all saved data (raising data, sleep records, etc.) will be completely deleted from the device. If you use cloud backup features, it depends on the specifications of your OS (iOS/Android).

4. Permissions

The App may request the following permissions to provide alarm and notification features.

  • Notifications (Local Notifications): Used to notify you of the pet's status and alarm times.

5. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may change this privacy policy as necessary. If changes occur, we will notify you within the App or on the app store page.
